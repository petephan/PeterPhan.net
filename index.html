About

Peter is an experienced Product Manager, QA Engineer, and has a proven track record of success in various roles within successful organizations that include ComplianceEase, as well as contract work with Google, Fitbit, Jiff, and McGraw-Hill.

He's also a Certified ScrumMaster and has a BS in Management Information Systems from San Jose State University. Outside of the office, he is a Codecademy and Udacity student for web and Android development, as well as a staunch cryptocurrency and Golden State Warriors enthusiast.
