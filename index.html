Our Company

For nearly 20 years, LiquidNation has helped people with their daily hydration needs. Our employees are passionate about creativity, quality and innovation, values that have guided us from the start and remain at our core. We're best known for drinking water, but we're much more than that. Our products include energy drinks, vitamin drinks, health drinks, IQ boosting drinks, muscle building drinks, drinks to get the creative juices flowing, even an all natural green drink made from recyled drinks from other drinks.

We're privately held, and we are devoted to finding the best ways to serve your needs. With leadership taking us into the 21st century. We've grown from a hole-in-the-wall drink stand into a $7.4 billion company. Our products can be found in 100 countries around the world and in more than 43,000 stores in the United States alone. Our flagship retail partners offer the widest selection of our products in communities across the U.S. Most of them are independently owned by local entrepreneurs who care about their communities as much as we do. Our roots extend deep in the Bay Area of California where our corporate headquarters is located. We have manufacturing and distribution facilities in New Jersey, The Bermuda Triangle, Himalayas, Sri Lanka and Mexico and are active in helping build healthy communities in the cities and towns where our employees live and work. Our subsidiaries are our partners in good corporate citizenship. Creativity, innovation, quality, and our sincere care have guided our decisions and earned us the trust of people who work with us. It's a trust we intend to keep, it's thirst we intend to quench.
